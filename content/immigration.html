IMMIGRATION

Ignoring the issues that immigration from South and Central America is a costly and fatal mistake. We must take swift action against the problems these groups bring to our nation. Failure to do so could aggravate an already out of control situation.

19x

Hispanics are 19 times more likely to be in a gang.

Many people claim that the very idea of immigrants committing felonies is racist. However Hispanics are imprisoned at a rate 2.9 times that of White people [1] while receiving shorter prison sentences for the same crimes [2]. This problem doesn’t go away with time either, the children of Hispanic immigrants have a higher crime rate than the immigrants themselves [1]. Hispanics are three times as likely as Whites to commit violent crime, and 19 times more likely to be in a gang [3].

"Nearly half of all Hispanic children are born out of wedlock."

The families that these immigrants bring are often broken and detriment the community around them. Nearly half of all Hispanic children are born out of wedlock [4]. The children of these families pursue and earn college degrees at extremely low rates; rates that haven’t changed in nearly two decades [4]. These demographic changes are so dramatic, that in states like California, the per capita incomes of family households are project to drop by a huge margin [4].
